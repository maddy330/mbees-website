Our Legacy

Precision Born from Passion

MBEES was founded on a singular belief: that the materials defining our built environment deserve the same attention to detail as the architecture they support. Over 15 years, we have developed expertise across steel, aluminum, and glass — delivering solutions to residential villas, corporate campuses, and industrial facilities across South India.

  • We source exclusively from certified steel mills with traceable 304 and 316-grade stock, ensuring every weld, bend, and finish meets international structural standards.
  • Our in-house PVD coating facility delivers durable gold, rose gold, and black finishes — not paints, but ionic-bonded surfaces that resist corrosion and UV degradation for decades.
  • Every project is managed from design drawing through site installation by our dedicated project engineers, ensuring zero gap between specification and execution.
  • We work closely with architects and interior designers, offering material samples, 3D mockups, and custom profile fabrication to realize any design vision.
